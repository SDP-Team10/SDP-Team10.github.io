Why Railly Clean?

Concept - Summarise what your system does, its target market and uses cases here Railly Clean is a train sanitising robot that clears and sanitises train tables while the train is out of transit to reduce the workload of cleaning staff Throughout the UK each year there are 1.7 billion passenger rail journeys Our vision is to augement the existing cleaning staff employed by rail operators with a robot workforce who would be able to speed up the process of cleaning and by reducing the number of repetetive tasks that cleaners have to carry out to allow them to deliver an improved passenger experience. Use cases? Once a train has finished its journeys for the day it needs to be cleaned.

Automate Cleaning

Reduce costs and save time by augmenting your current cleaning team with a Railly Clean robot

  • ✓ Reduced risk of virus transmisson through reduced human contact
  • ✓ Cleaners can focus on thorough cleaning of other more complex areas
  • ✓ Faster cleaning time means more train uptime

Valuable Detection

Using our state of the art classification system, Railly clean can intellegently distinguish between valuables left on the train and rubbish to be cleared

  • ✓ Fewer items for cleaners to deal with
  • ✓ Integrated bin stores rubbish
